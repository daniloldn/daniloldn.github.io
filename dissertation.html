Resilience in complete financial networks: Key characteristics.

About this dissertation

This Undergraduate dissertation explores what characteristics of a fully connected network make it more resilient to exogenous shock. It focuses on financial networks and simulates GDP shocks (iid) to countries. It looks at the channels banks can be exposed to these shocks; through direct exposure or indirect exposure via the interbank market.
